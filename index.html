About

Built with purpose,
designed with care.

"I noticed a real discrepancy between the resources available for young adults starting their fitness journey versus those available for senior citizens — especially people with mobility challenges, who are often left out entirely. With multiple years of experience in bodyweight training and strength conditioning, I felt compelled to do something about it. I've volunteered as a fitness instructor for a chair-based exercise class in Austin, TX, and saw firsthand how much seniors benefited from workouts tailored specifically to their circumstances — something that current apps simply don't do. That's how Active Aging was born. The majority of workouts on this app are custom-made with easy instructions and a UI designed with senior citizens in mind."

Every design decision, from the large buttons to the voice coaching, was intentional.

— Gautam Penna, Creator of Active Aging
